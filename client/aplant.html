Tomato

132 g CO2e saved per tomato | Gardening Difficulty: Hard

Tomatoes are America’s favorite garden vegetable. This vine plant is fairly easy to grow and will produce a bumper crop with proper care. Its uses are versatile, however, tomatoes are susceptible to a range of pests and diseases.

Plant Care

Supplies: pot, soil, tomato seeds, water

Watering: Make sure soil remains moist but is well-drained

Sunlight: Full Sun (at least 6 hours a day)

Soil: Loamy, Acidic pH

Timing: Wait 5-6 weeks after the last frost date to transplant outdoors. For optimal germination, keep the soil at around 80-85°F.
